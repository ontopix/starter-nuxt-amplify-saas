## The challenge

Property and facility management organizations face a unique operational burden: they must serve multiple stakeholders—tenants, property owners, contractors, and public administrations—while balancing efficiency, compliance, and customer satisfaction. Calls and emails often cover recurring issues such as maintenance requests, payment clarifications, or scheduling of common-area services. Peaks of demand are unpredictable: a sudden plumbing issue or an electricity outage can overwhelm phone lines in minutes.

Traditionally, solving this requires onboarding large support teams, who then spend much of their time handling repetitive, low-value interactions. Staff turnover adds to the problem: training new employees is costly, and consistency in service quality is difficult to maintain. Tenants expect fast, clear communication, but backlogs, waiting times, and unresolved tickets are frequent pain points.

## Ontopix's approach

Ontopix Virtual Agents are designed to act as the first line of communication in property and facility management. They operate 24/7, handling voice and chat interactions with natural language capabilities that allow them to understand requests, provide instant answers, and trigger workflows directly in facility management systems.

Whether it is scheduling a repair, providing payment status, or giving building access instructions, the virtual agent integrates seamlessly with CRMs, ERPs, and property management platforms. Complex cases that require human intervention are automatically escalated with full context, ensuring property managers receive complete, structured information.

By automating routine queries and requests, Ontopix not only reduces operational costs but also frees human staff to focus on high-value interactions, such as negotiations with contractors or resolving disputes.

## Benefits in practice

* **Faster response times:** Tenants receive immediate confirmation of their requests, reducing frustration and uncertainty.
* **24/7 availability:** Emergency issues can be reported at any hour, without requiring night-shift teams.
* **Operational efficiency:** Routine calls—such as “When is my next payment due?” or “How do I book the community hall?”—are resolved instantly by the agent.
* **Consistent communication:** Virtual agents deliver standardized, compliant information every time, avoiding errors or misunderstandings.
* **Lower onboarding costs:** New staff can focus on complex tasks rather than repetitive training for routine issues.

## A day-to-day scenario

A tenant notices a water leak in their apartment late at night. Instead of waiting until office hours or leaving a voicemail, they call the facility's support number. The Ontopix Virtual Agent answers immediately, gathers the essential details, checks the maintenance calendar, and schedules a plumber for the next morning. Simultaneously, it notifies the property manager and generates a work order in the facility's management system.

The tenant receives instant reassurance and a confirmation message, while the human property manager starts the day already informed, with priorities set. What used to be a stressful and delayed process now becomes smooth, predictable, and transparent.

## Why it matters

Facility and property administration is not just about managing buildings—it is about building trust with the people who live or work in them. Responsiveness, clarity, and reliability define the tenant experience and strongly influence long-term satisfaction and retention.

By deploying Ontopix Virtual Agents, property managers transform their communication model: from reactive, overloaded, and inconsistent to proactive, scalable, and professional. The result is not only happier tenants and owners but also leaner operations and improved competitiveness in a market where customer experience is as valuable as location or price.
