## The challenge

Administrative processes are the backbone of any organization, yet they are often burdened by complexity, strict regulations, and heavy documentation. Teams responsible for accounting, fiscal compliance, or audit preparation spend countless hours searching through policies, gathering supporting evidence, and responding to recurring internal questions.

This repetitive effort is not only time-consuming but also prone to human error, especially under the pressure of audits or regulatory deadlines. Employees often need immediate clarification on procedures, reporting standards, or required documentation—yet waiting for a colleague's response can cause bottlenecks and frustration. In many cases, knowledge is dispersed across manuals, emails, and systems, making it hard to ensure consistency and compliance.

Organizations face a double challenge: keeping costs under control while meeting high standards of accuracy and transparency, particularly in regulated domains such as finance, accounting, and taxation.

## Ontopix's approach

Ontopix Virtual Agents are not limited to customer-facing use cases—they can also become a powerful ally for internal teams. By embedding company policies, accounting procedures, and fiscal guidelines into the agent's knowledge base, employees gain instant, 24/7 access to reliable information.

When preparing for audits, for instance, the agent can guide staff step-by-step through the required documentation, remind them of reporting deadlines, or even validate that certain procedures comply with internal and external requirements. Through natural language interactions, employees simply ask their questions—such as *“Which VAT regulation applies here?”* or *“What evidence is needed for this transaction?”*—and the agent provides precise, contextual answers.

Thanks to integrations with existing systems (ERP, accounting software, document repositories), Ontopix ensures the answers are not generic but specific to the company's data and policies. The virtual agent can also escalate more complex or exceptional cases to the right specialist, ensuring no request is left unresolved.

## Benefits in practice

* **Efficiency gains**: Employees spend less time searching for information or chasing colleagues for answers.
* **Consistency and accuracy**: Every employee receives the same, up-to-date guidance aligned with company policies and regulatory frameworks.
* **Audit readiness**: Documentation and process requirements can be retrieved instantly, reducing stress and errors during inspections.
* **Cost optimization**: Instead of onboarding additional administrative staff, organizations empower existing teams with scalable, intelligent support.
* **24/7 availability**: Even outside regular office hours, employees can resolve doubts and move forward with their work.

## A day-to-day scenario

Imagine an accounting team preparing for a quarterly audit. Traditionally, the process involves dozens of emails, cross-checks, and manual reviews. A junior accountant might hesitate, unsure whether a specific invoice requires additional supporting documents for compliance. Normally, they would wait hours—sometimes days—for a senior colleague's clarification.

With Ontopix, the same employee can simply ask the virtual agent: *“Does this invoice over €5,000 need extra approval under our policy?”* The agent instantly provides the correct procedure, referencing the relevant regulation and pointing to the document template stored in the ERP system. If needed, it alerts the employee to submit it for final validation.

Instead of delays, the process flows seamlessly. Senior staff can focus on exceptions and strategic analysis, while the bulk of recurring questions are resolved automatically.

Internal efficiency is just as critical as customer satisfaction. Companies that streamline administrative processes not only save costs but also strengthen compliance, reduce risks, and increase employee satisfaction.

Ontopix Virtual Agents help organizations transform internal operations from a time-consuming necessity into a competitive advantage.
