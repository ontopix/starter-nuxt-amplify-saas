## The challenge

Financial institutions face a dual expectation: deliver exceptional service while ensuring strict compliance with regulations. Customers rely on their bank or financial provider for highly sensitive matters—fraud alerts, card issues, balance inquiries, or suspicious activity. Yet the volume of these interactions is enormous, and the stakes are high.

When clients cannot get immediate assistance, anxiety escalates quickly. A blocked card during travel, an unexpected charge, or a suspected fraud attempt are moments when reassurance must be instant. Traditional call centers, however, struggle to meet these needs. Long waiting times, limited hours, and high onboarding and training costs create a service gap that undermines both trust and efficiency.

The industry cannot afford slow responses. Every delayed interaction risks both customer satisfaction and compliance obligations.

## Ontopix's approach

Ontopix Virtual Agents bring immediate, intelligent, and compliant support to banking and finance. Designed to handle sensitive topics with accuracy and empathy, they provide 24/7 coverage across voice and text channels.

These agents understand natural language, detect intent, and instantly access customer records through secure integrations with banking systems. Whether a customer is checking their balance, reporting a stolen card, or seeking clarification on a transaction, the virtual agent responds instantly, guided by workflows aligned with regulatory requirements such as GDPR, SOC2, or HIPAA (for health-related financial services).

Importantly, Ontopix Virtual Agents do not replace human expertise where it matters. They triage, resolve routine inquiries, and seamlessly escalate complex or high-risk cases to live agents—providing full conversation history for smooth handover.

## A day-to-day scenario

Imagine a customer abroad whose debit card is suddenly blocked after a suspicious transaction. It is midnight in their local time zone, and panic sets in. Instead of waiting for the next call center shift or spending 30 minutes on hold, the customer connects immediately with an Ontopix Virtual Agent.

The agent confirms the customer’s identity, explains the reason for the block, and initiates a card replacement request. It also reassures the customer by sending confirmation details via SMS and email. If necessary, the case is escalated to a fraud specialist, who receives the full transcript and system logs—ready to act without making the customer repeat their story.

What once took hours is now resolved in minutes, with full compliance and peace of mind.
